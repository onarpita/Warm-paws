# WarmPaws – Pet Care in Winter

**WarmPaws** is a cozy winter companion platform for pet owners to ensure their furry friends stay warm, safe, and healthy during the cold season.

## Live Link

[Live Site](https://warm-paws.netlify.app/)  

## Features

**Winter Pet Services:** Find local grooming, boarding, and veterinary services.
**Pet Clothing & Accessories:** Explore warm outfits and protective gear for pets.
**Care Tips:** Expert advice on keeping pets safe in cold weather.
**Expert Vets:** Access guidance from certified veterinarians.
**Interactive Hero Carousel:** Engaging landing section with seasonal highlights.
**User Authentication:** Sign up, log in, and manage profiles securely.

## Project Purpose

WarmPaws helps pet owners find winter care services, expert tips, and cozy accessories for their pets.  
Users can browse services, view protected details, and book services after login.


## Extra Sections
